# StarNet Cafe Business Analytics Project

This project aims to optimize retail operations for Star Net Cafe.

### Business Context: The Reality of Operations in Yemen
Operating a retail business like Star Net Cafe demands more than standard accounting; it requires a deep understanding of operational resilience. In a volatile environment where energy stability is not guaranteed, our profit margins are continuously tested.

### Strategic Objectives
- **Energy-Adjusted Profitability:** We are shifting focus from simple revenue metrics to "Net-Energy Profitability."
- **Data-Driven Decision Making:** This project is a field-based effort to document the correlation between sales and power availability.
- **Operational Optimization:** My goal is to prioritize high-margin products during high-cost generator hours.

### Advanced Data Analysis
I have developed a SQL query to calculate "Net Profit Estimates" by applying a 20% operational overhead to transactions processed during generator hours.

### Future Vision
This project is the first phase of digital transformation, with future plans for automated dashboards.
